Info
  • sections like this one on Info can be expanded/collapsed by clicking on their titles (note the arrow/triangle)
  • click the toggle auto spin button to enable/disable spinning the camera around the scene
  • left-click and drag horizontally on the canvas to rotate your view of the scene (or adjust the view angle slider)
  • left-click and drag vertically on the canvas to move your view up and down
  • scroll with your cursor over the canvas to zoom in and out
  • manually rotating the camera with disable the auto rotation
  • click the radio buttons on the headings under Animators to switch animation (or switch to manual control mode)
  • (shift-clicking switches to one of the animations, so if you're already on the one it switches to nothing will change)
  • a debug overlay showing the model's armature can be enabled under Misc
