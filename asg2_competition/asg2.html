How To Use
  • sections like this one can be expanded/collapsed by clicking on their titles (note the arrow/triangle)
  • to see the model's armature, enable "render bones" under Debug Overlays
  • camera control:
    • click the toggle auto spin button to enable/disable spinning the camera around the scene
    • left-click and drag horizontally on the canvas to rotate your view of the scene (or adjust the view angle slider)
    • left-click and drag vertically on the canvas to move your view up and down
    • middle-click and drag vertically on the canvas (or alt + left-click if you don't have a middle mouse button) to aim the camera up/down
    • scroll with your cursor over the canvas to zoom in and out
    • (manually rotating the camera with disable the auto rotation)
  • animation control:
    • click the radio buttons () on the headings under Animation Control to switch animation (or switch to manual control mode)
    • (shift-clicking just switches to a specific animation, so if you're already on that one nothing will change)
